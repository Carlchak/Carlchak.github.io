ABOUT


【Ambition】
Every cabinet member of us is indispensable. Everyone works together and helps each other, with the goal of serving schoolmates and the school.
The name of our cabinet is composed of 6 English letters, which represent different words, which bring out the ambition and policy of our cabinet.

Enthusiastic: We uphold an unquenchable enthusiasm and work hard to do everything. As the saying goes, " They who love it are better than those who know it." Our cabinet believes that the enthusiasm for the duties of the Student Union can make us work better.💪

Unique: We hope to organize more new activities and services with innovative ideas, provide students with different conveniences, and become a unique student union in KTGSS😄

Receptive: We are willing to listen and accept all the opinions from all the parties. A government of a country must listen and accept the opinions of the public to succeed. We are willing to listen to all kinds of voices, improve ourselves, and build a better campus.

Earnest: We work rigorously and seriously, seeking truth from facts. We believe that only solemn attitude, honesty and integrity can make a student union qualified.✊

Keen: We work quickly, quickly collect the opinions and needs from schoolmates, and put them into practice to help schoolmates solve their needs, and make it more convenient and more enjoyable for schoolmates in school.😆

Aspiring: We hope we can take the campus to the next level.

We believe that the student union should have a broad mind and a firm will. We will take the responsibility for the entire school🙌
